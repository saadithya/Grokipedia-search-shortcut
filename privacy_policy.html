Privacy Policy

Grokipedia Search Shortcut

Overview

The Grokipedia Search Shortcut Chrome extension values your privacy. This document explains how we handle your data and ensure your browsing experience remains secure and private.

Information Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information about its users. All searches performed through the extension are processed directly by Grokipedia’s public search engine in your own browser.

Permissions Used

  • contextMenus: Allows the extension to add the “Search Grokipedia for…” option when text is selected.
  • omnibox: Enables the grok keyword search directly from the browser’s address bar.

These permissions are strictly necessary for the extension’s functionality and are not used for tracking or analytics.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

The extension does not transmit data to any external servers, nor does it share information with third parties. All interactions occur locally in your browser.

Cookies and Analytics

The Grokipedia Search Shortcut does not use cookies, analytics tools, or tracking scripts of any kind.

Third-Party Services

When you perform a search using this extension, your query is directed to grokipedia.com. Please note that Grokipedia’s website may have its own privacy practices, which are outside the control of this extension.

Disclaimer

This extension is an independent project and is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Grokipedia or Grok. It simply acts as a user convenience tool to make Grokipedia searches more accessible.
